What is Open Hamilton?

We're a group of Hamilton citizens who want to make the city better using open data.

What is Open data?

From the WikiPedia article, "Open data is the idea that certain data should be freely available to everyone to use and republish as they wish, without restrictions from copyright, patents or other mechanisms of control."

Dowsing in Hamilton

Get the mobile version here!

Dowsing helps you find beaches, pools, splash pads and water parks in Hamilton to cool off from the summer heat. If we've missed any water spots let us know by going to our Google Group. Dowsing takes its name from the practice of the using two rods to find water underground. (Wikipedia)

Put it on your website!

You can embed Dowsing on your own site with just 2 lines of code. Put the following code where you want the widget to be displayed on your site: 

<script type="text/javascript">
var DowsingConfig = { height: 500, width: 500 };
document.write(unescape("%3Cscript src='http://openhamilton.ca/dowsing/desktop/dowsing.js' type='text/javascript'%3E%3C/script%3E"));
</script>
