Dowsing - Hamilton's Swimming App (mobile version)

Dowsing helps you find beaches, pools, splash pads and water parks in Hamilton to cool off from the summer heat. If we've missed any water spots let us know by going to our Google Group. Dowsing takes its name from the practice of the using two rods to find water underground. (Wikipedia)

Embedding Dowsing

You can embed Dowsing on your own site with just 2 lines of code. Put the following code where you want the Dowsing widget to be displayed on your site: 

<script type="text/javascript">
var DowsingConfig = { height: 500, width: 500 };
document.write(unescape("%3Cscript src='http://openhamilton.ca/dowsing/desktop/dowsing.js' type='text/javascript'%3E%3C/script%3E"));
</script>
You can change the height and width of the widget by changing the values on the second line. The minimum height is 400 and the minimum width is 500. If you encounter any problems with the widget let us know by going to our Google Group and we'll help you get the issue sorted out.
